About me

I was an Instructor at Butler University (BU) up until May 2020.

I defended my Ph.D. thesis in May 2019 at Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis (IUPUI).
My advisor was Dr. Rodrigo Pérez.

I received bachelor and master degrees in Mathematics from Boston University (BU) in 2013.

I am a co-founder of the AMS Student Chapter at IUPUI and held the president position from Spring 2014 to Fall 2015.


Hobbies

I enjoy juggling, coding (mostly in Mathematica, Processing, p5js), making origami, and, most importantly, playing with animals.
